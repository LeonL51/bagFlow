Key Features 

1. Smart Spending Analysis
 The app organizes expenses into clear categories and identifies spending patterns over time. Students can easily see where their money is going each month and how their habits compare across weeks or semesters. This feature helps users recognize areas of overspending that may otherwise go unnoticed.

2. Predictive Budget Forecasting
 Based on recorded income and past spending behavior, the app estimates how much money a student is likely to have remaining by the end of the month. If spending trends indicate a potential shortfall, the app provides early warnings so users can adjust before financial stress occurs.

3. Subscription and Recurring Expense Monitoring
 The app tracks recurring payments such as streaming services, memberships, and software subscriptions. It highlights how small monthly charges accumulate into larger annual costs, helping students make informed decisions about which services are truly necessary.

4. Goal-Oriented Saving and Progress Tracking
 Students can set specific financial goals, such as saving for textbooks, rent, or personal expenses. The app visually tracks progress toward these goals and provides feedback that encourages consistent saving habits.
